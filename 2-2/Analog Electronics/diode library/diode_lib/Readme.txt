********************************************************************************
If OrCAD 16.6 Lite is installed in your computer, add the following libraries. *
Note that the paths may be different for your computer.                        *
                                                                               *
********************************************************************************
Copy "diode.lib" and "nomd.lib"                                                *
Open the folder: C:\OrCAD\OrCAD_16.6_Lite\tools\pspice\library                 *
Paste them and be sure they are in the folder.                                 *
(Click "Replace the file..." for "nomd.lib")                                   *                                            *
********************************************************************************      
                                                                               *
Copy "diode.olb"                                                               *
Open the folder: C:\OrCAD\OrCAD_16.6_Lite\tools\capture\library\pspice         *
Paste it and be sure "diode.olb" is in the folder.                             *
********************************************************************************
                                                                               *
Now open a project and add "diode" library. You can pick a diode and use it on *
your schematic.                                                                *
********************************************************************************
